❖ Biography ❖


College

Integration of Information Technology is a new trend in various subjects of modern society. Although my major in college was bio-related, I took courses in programming and information security informatics and participated many workshops to improve my skills in Informatics.

My first project in bioinformatics was a classification problem on human microbiome data. The results have been published in the18th IEEE International Conference on Bioinformatics and Bioengineering. Another similar approach was applied to predict the probability of BMI. The resulted was submitted to the International Journal of Uncertainty and Innovation.

Graduated School

In graduated school, I took various courses related to biomedical informatics, such as Programming with python, Data analyzing, Deep learning, Biostatistics, and Translational medical informatics. Gradually, I become familiar with basic data analyzing skills and applications of informatics technique in clinical environment. These trainings help me to take the first step to be a biomedical data scientist.

Summer Internship

In the first summer of the graduated school, I got an opportunity to go to the NIH (US) for an internship. During this period of time, in addition to improve my skills on programming and problem solving, I also started my master research topic in building a data cleaning and validating tool. This tool is able to generate machine learning suitable material from clinical electronic health record data. Although the current model can only partially improve the data quality, I still learned a lot from the development progress.

Future

After graduated, I hope I can make good use of what I have learned, and solve more advanced tasks in the future.




❖ Research Interests ❖

My research interests are in Bioinformatics, Health record data processing, Machine learning, Human microbiome and Line chat bot.
In addition to biomedical data analysis, I am also willing to try other researching methods and topics, such as computer image processing, deep learning applications, etc.
